Grounded - For innovative movement project

 

"For my innovative movement project, I wanted to recreate a kind of POV shot but from another angle, rather than just eye level. After considering ways of incorporating a high mode POV shot, I decided to do a low mode shot instead because I had more tools accessible to me to achieve a shot from below. I originally wanted to focus on reflections; how water reflects whatever it mirrors, which is why much of my video shows puddles of water. But as I walked through my location with my camera, I realized how the ground reflected light, and its effect on the shots I took, so I chose to incorporate that as well. I decided to capture other angles of the objects that the water would pick up/reflect (you may notice this as I take extreme low shots of buildings that reappear in water puddles)." -Gabi, Dec. 2016
